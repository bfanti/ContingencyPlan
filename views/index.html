WHY?

Are you an avid adventurer? Do you create your own foot steps instead of following others?
Ski mountaineering, hiking, or trail running off the beaten path?
All those activities are high risk, high reward activities, and let's be honest, shit happens.
Have you ever left your loved ones at home with only approximate details about your adventure, and wondered "What if I get hurt? Will SAR know where to look for me?"

WHAT?

How Contingency Plans helps you...

ADVENTURE

Create an Adventure plan with as much detail as you want: start date and time, start coordinates, waypoint coordinates or full GPS route, description, and estimated time of return.

CONTINGENCY

Think something might go wrong? Attach a Contingency Plan to your Adventure! Define expected stages of contingency and desired escalation, then connect with your closests friends and loved ones by e-mail, SMS, or phone.
